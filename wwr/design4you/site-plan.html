    [Services]

    [Our website mainly provides 3 designs for you to desplay]

      Web Design
      We help you build your own website. Including information architecture, user interface, website structure, navigation, layout, color, font and overall effect presentation.
    • Layout: A layout is composed of images, advertisements and text. In the online world, the most important thing is to allow users to find the information they need at the first glance. This requires maintaining the coherence, consistency and integrity of the design.
    • Color: The choice of color is based on the purpose of the website and customers. It can range from very simple black and white to multi-color design. Common colors in web pages can convey the characteristics of characters and brands.
    • Images: Images include logos, photographs, illustrations or symbols, which can enhance web design. These need to be properly designed with page colors and content for the convenience of the user, while not overly cluttering the whole or slowing down the page.
    • Fonts: Using different fonts can enhance web design even more. Most browsers can only support a small number of fonts, which are called web-safe fonts, which means that web designers tend to use fonts that are generally supported by browsers.
    • Content: Content and design can enhance a website's message through both text and visuals. The text on the website needs to be relevant and valid so as not to mislead users into wrong information. Content should be search engine compliant with appropriate text length and relevant keywords.
      Advertising Design
      The design is mainly designed for the purpose of strengthening sales, and the purpose is to advertise your products, brands, activities, etc. through advertising and design.
    • Billboard Advertisement:Using computer design and printing (or computer direct printing), the picture is eye-catching and lifelike, with a strong three-dimensional effect, which reproduces the charm of the product, and is most effective in establishing the urban image of the product (brand), and is easy to post and replace. The materials used are also rain-proof and sun-proof.
    • Neonlight Advertisement:Neon lights are one of the main forms of lighting advertisements in outdoor advertising. Its media characteristics are to use new technologies, new methods, and new materials to attract the attention of the audience with light, color, and dynamic characteristics in the form of expression, thereby improving information. acceptance rate. Neon sign advertisements are generally set in eye-catching positions such as the highest point of the city, the roof of a building and the facade of a store.
    • Public Transportation Advertisement:Since public transport vehicles travel to and from the main streets in the city center, advertising on both sides of the vehicle or on the front and rear of the vehicle will cover a wide range and the advertising effect will be particularly strong. Most of these outdoor advertisements still use the traditional form of paint painting, combined with some methods of computer printing and pasting.
    • Light Box Advertisement:The media characteristics of light box advertisements, lampposts, tower advertisements, street clock advertisements and bus shelter advertisements all use light to illuminate light sheets, posters, and flexible materials to form single-sided, double-sided, three-sided or four-sided light advertisements. This kind of advertisement has beautiful appearance, simple picture and good visual effect.
      Animation Design
      Animation design is mainly based on the production of related aspects such as online games, animation animation production, advertising planning production, etc., to provide you with commercial design.
    • 2D animation: Two-dimensional design, also known as graphic design, is a design activity based on the long and wide two-dimensional space, covering a wide range, including posters, sample binding, book design, VI, picture album, etc. as long as it is in two-dimensional space The design carried out in the website belongs to the category of graphic design.
    • 3D animation: The way 3D animation technology mimics real objects makes it a useful tool. Due to its accuracy, authenticity and operability, it is widely used in many fields such as medicine, education, military affairs, and entertainment. In terms of film and television advertising production, this new technology can give people a refreshing feeling, so it has been welcomed by many customers. 3D animation can be used in the production of special effects (such as explosions, smoke, rain, light effects, etc.), special effects (crash, deformation, illusory scenes or characters, etc.), advertising product display, opening titles, etc. in advertisements and movies and TV series.
